# NOZOMI Economy Design

# Core Philosophy

The economy is progression-driven.

The purpose of the economy is to:
- reinforce mastery
- reinforce consistency
- pace progression
- maintain long-term motivation

The economy must NEVER feel:
- exploitative
- grindy
- casino-like
- manipulative

---

# XP Economy

XP represents:
- growth
- understanding
- mastery

XP is earned through:
- quests
- dungeons
- speech encounters
- conversations
- raid participation

---

# XP Scaling Philosophy

Early levels:
- fast progression
- rapid onboarding
- quick feedback

Mid levels:
- increasing challenge
- stronger mastery requirements

Late levels:
- meaningful investment
- specialization
- long-term progression

---

# Reward Philosophy

Rewards should:
- feel earned
- reinforce gameplay loops
- encourage mastery

Rewards should NOT:
- overwhelm the player
- spam dopamine
- trivialize progression

---

# Dungeon Economy

Dungeon rewards scale through:
- difficulty
- preparation
- survival
- encounter completion
- hidden events

Dungeon rewards should reward:
- strategy
- adaptation
- consistency

Not:
- repetitive farming

---

# Penalty Economy

Penalties slow progression temporarily.

Penalties should:
- create tension
- encourage improvement
- increase emotional stakes

Penalties must NEVER:
- permanently cripple players
- destroy momentum completely

---

# Rank Economy

Hunter ranks:
- E
- D
- C
- B
- A
- S

Ranks represent:
- mastery
- consistency
- progression status

Ranks should feel prestigious.

---

# Unlock Philosophy

Unlocks should:
- feel meaningful
- expand gameplay possibilities
- increase immersion

Avoid:
- meaningless filler unlocks
- reward clutter
- unnecessary currencies

---

# Anti-Grind Philosophy

The game should reward:
- understanding
- adaptation
- consistency

The game should NOT reward:
- mindless repetition
- macro behavior
- exploit farming

---

# Long-Term Economy Goals

The economy should support:
- years of progression
- expanding systems
- new dungeons
- multiplayer raids
- evolving mastery

The economy must remain scalable.

---

# Final Principle

Progression should feel:
“I became stronger.”

Not:
“I repeated chores long enough.”
