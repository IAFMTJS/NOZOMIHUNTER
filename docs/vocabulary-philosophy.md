# Vocabulary Philosophy

Vocabulary is progression.

The player should never feel:
- interrupted constantly
- trapped in lessons
- forced into tutorials

Instead:
- vocabulary gets introduced before quests
- quests reinforce vocabulary naturally
- mastery grows through usage

---

# Core Philosophy

Preparation happens BEFORE gameplay tension.

The player:
- studies briefly
- prepares mentally
- enters the encounter ready

This creates:
- immersion
- anticipation
- strategic preparation

---

# Vocabulary During Quests

During gameplay:
- words reappear naturally
- no excessive explanations appear
- the world reinforces meaning contextually

The dungeon itself becomes reinforcement.

---

# Mastery Philosophy

Exposure alone is not mastery.

Track:
- recognition
- understanding
- recall speed
- confidence
- hesitation
- contextual usage

---

# Preparation Philosophy

Preparation should feel like:
- mission briefing
- hunter preparation
- strategic planning

Not:
- homework
- flashcards
- classroom study

Human brains learn surprisingly well when they think they're surviving fictional danger. Very normal species behavior.
