# NOZOMI HUNTER — SHOP & ECONOMY SYSTEM

## Purpose

The Shop System exists to:

- Create emotional progression loops
- Give players meaningful temporary power
- Increase long-term retention
- Add strategic decision making
- Create reward anticipation
- Support dungeon progression
- Reinforce the “Hunter System” fantasy

The economy must never feel like a normal mobile shop.

It should feel like:
- a dangerous enhancement market
- a hunter black market
- a forbidden system terminal
- a shadow dealer interface

The player should feel:
> “I am preparing for my next raid.”

NOT:
> “I am buying mobile game consumables.”

---

# CORE CURRENCIES

## 1. XP (Experience)

XP is the primary progression resource.

Used for:
- leveling
- ranking
- stat growth
- unlock systems
- prestige progression
- hunter evolution

XP should feel valuable and difficult to obtain at higher ranks.

XP is NOT the primary shop currency.

---

## 2. Credits

Credits are the main shop currency.

Used for:
- temporary boosts
- consumables
- utility items
- dungeon survival tools
- quest modifiers
- cosmetic enhancements

Credits are earned through:
- dungeon completion
- boss rewards
- achievements
- daily quests
- level up rewards
- event rewards
- XP conversion

---

## 3. Premium Crystals (Future System)

Reserved for:
- cosmetics
- ultra rare effects
- account customization
- special event items

Must NOT become pay-to-win.

This currency may support monetization later.

---

# XP → CREDIT CONVERSION

XP can be exchanged into Credits.

However:
- conversion is intentionally inefficient
- prevents economy abuse
- prevents easy quest farming
- forces strategic decision making

---

## Example Conversion Rates

| XP | Credits |
|---|---|
| 100 XP | 10 Credits |
| 1000 XP | 70 Credits |
| 5000 XP | 250 Credits |

---

## Additional Balancing Options

### Conversion Tax
A percentage of XP is lost during conversion.

Example:
- 1000 XP converted
- only 700 XP value received

---

### Daily Conversion Limit
Restricts economy inflation.

Example:
- 3 conversions per day
- or max daily conversion cap

---

### Lore Integration

The conversion system should feel dangerous.

Example system message:

```text
Converting XP destabilizes your Hunter Core.
Proceed?
```

This makes balancing feel immersive instead of artificial.

# SHOP STRUCTURE

The shop is divided into categories.

---

# COMBAT BOOSTS

Temporary enhancements that assist gameplay without removing skill requirements.

---

## Unlimited Mistakes

### Effect
For 15 minutes:
- mistakes do not reduce HP
- streaks are protected
- failure penalties disabled

### Purpose
- panic recovery
- confidence building
- challenge pushing

### Notes
- timer continues outside quests
- does NOT pause

### Suggested Cost
250–400 Credits

---

## XP Booster

### Effect
Temporarily increases XP gain.

### Variants

#### Small Booster
+25% XP for 15 min

#### Major Booster
+50% XP for 30 min

#### Insane Booster
+100% XP for limited duration

### Notes
- stack prevention recommended
- event versions possible

---

## Stat Buffer

Temporary stat enhancement.

### Possible Buffs
- +INT
- +MEMORY
- +FOCUS
- +SPEED
- +REACTION
- +STAMINA

### Gameplay Effects
- easier timed challenges
- larger combo windows
- slower concentration drain
- improved dungeon performance

---

## Rank Shield

### Effect
Prevents rank loss after failure.

### Usage
High-tier risk protection.

### Psychological Purpose
Reduces fear of difficult content while preserving tension.

---

# QUEST MANIPULATION ITEMS

These are high-value strategic items.

Rare and expensive.

---

## Difficulty Override

### Item Name
System Breach

### Effect
Allows:
- Hard Quest rewards
- while playing Easy Quest difficulty

### Restrictions
- limited uses
- very high cost
- cannot be used on special bosses
- disabled in ranked competitive modes

### Purpose
Allows progression assistance without removing reward excitement.

---

## Reward Amplifier

### Effect
Temporarily doubles:
- dungeon rewards
- credits
- loot chance

### Duration
Single dungeon or timed session.

---

## Quest Retry Ticket

### Effect
Instantly retry failed quest.

### Removes
- cooldowns
- waiting timers
- recovery penalties

---

## Skip Token

### Effect
Skips:
- one objective
- one subtask
- one puzzle phase

### Restrictions
- cannot skip boss fights
- cannot skip final objectives

### Purpose
Prevents frustration during long quest chains.

---

# DUNGEON UTILITY ITEMS

High-pressure survival tools.

---

## Revive Token

### Effect
One additional revive during dungeon runs.

### Restrictions
- max use per dungeon
- unavailable in hardcore modes

---

## Escape Beacon

### Effect
Allows emergency dungeon escape.

### Removes
- death penalties
- heavy XP loss
- failed run punishments

### Cost
Should remain expensive.

---

## Time Freeze

### Effect
Pauses dungeon timer temporarily.

### Example
30 seconds frozen time.

### Restrictions
- high cost
- limited inventory carry amount

# IMPORTANT BALANCING RULES

## NEVER Remove Skill Completely

Boosts should:
- assist players
- reduce frustration
- encourage attempts

Boosts should NOT:
- autoplay content
- trivialize hard mechanics
- invalidate progression

---

## Temporary Power > Permanent Power

Permanent stat inflation destroys progression pacing.

Temporary boosts:
- preserve balance
- create strategic spending
- encourage resource management

---

# DUNGEON-EXCLUSIVE ITEMS

Some items should only be obtainable from:
- bosses
- hidden rooms
- elite dungeons
- special events

This creates:
- prestige
- rarity
- excitement
- replay motivation

Not everything should be purchasable.
