# Vocabulary Preparation Flow

Quest Generated
↓
Extract Quest Vocabulary
↓
Compare With Player Mastery
↓
Detect Unknown Words
↓
Prioritize Critical Vocabulary
↓
Generate Mission Preparation Briefing
↓
Player Reviews Vocabulary
↓
Preparation Score Calculated
↓
`/prepare` deploy gate (ring gauge, score breakdown, threat intel, blocker panel + checklist; deploy blocked until every checklist row is complete including operational readiness ≥ 35% and mission vocabulary ≥ 60% when applicable)
↓
Quest Starts
↓
Natural Reinforcement During Gameplay
↓
Mastery Tracking Updated

---

# Philosophy

Vocabulary learning should happen:
- before tension
- before encounters
- before pressure

The quest itself should reinforce mastery naturally.

The quest should NEVER pause constantly for explanations.

---

# Preparation Goals

The preparation phase exists to:
- reduce confusion
- preserve immersion
- reinforce mastery
- create anticipation

The player should feel:
- prepared
- informed
- immersed

Not:
- interrupted
- overloaded
- trapped in tutorials

---

# Vocabulary Priority Rules

Only explain:
- critical objective words
- survival vocabulary
- important NPC vocabulary
- dungeon mechanic terms
- boss mechanic terms

Avoid explaining:
- every unknown word
- low relevance filler vocabulary

---

# Gameplay Integration

During the quest:
- previously explained words reappear naturally
- mastery gets reinforced contextually
- AI tracks recognition speed
- AI tracks confidence
- AI tracks hesitation

The player should feel:
“I prepared for this mission.”

Not:
“I opened a dictionary simulator.”
